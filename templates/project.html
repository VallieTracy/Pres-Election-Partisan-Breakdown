Mapping the Election

In this project we got the data from this website. Usually, maps just display blue or red if the Democratic or Republican is the winner. We decided to do the coloring on a scale to show the spread of votes.

The main page shows the map of the great, beautiful USA, but state, and then you can view the county detail from each state. The MN Precinct Map page shows the votes by precinct. We chose to focus on MN since we all live in MN.
